# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

HabitTrack ("the App") is committed to privacy by design.

## Summary

- The App is fully open-source.
- The App works 100% offline.
- The App stores data only on your device using a local SQLite database via Room.
- The App does **not** collect, transmit, monetize, sell, rent, or share personal data.
- The App does **not** use analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, trackers, or third-party data collection services.

## What Data the App Uses

The App stores only the habit information you enter (for example: habit names, notes, completion records) locally on your device.

This local data is used only to provide app functionality (habit tracking, calendar status, and widget summary).

## Data Collection and Sharing

HabitTrack does not:

- collect personal information from you,
- send your data to any server,
- share your data with third parties,
- monetize your data in any way.

## Data Storage and Control

All app data remains on your device unless you manually export, back up, or share it using tools outside the App.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored app data may be deleted by your operating system according to platform behavior.

## Children’s Privacy

Because HabitTrack does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Open-Source Transparency

HabitTrack is open-source, so its behavior can be reviewed in source code form.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the project repository with a revised effective date.
